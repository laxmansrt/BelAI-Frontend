⚡ Quick Farming Tips

🌱 Soil Health

• Test soil every 2 years at KVK
• Add organic compost (FYM) before planting
• Rotate crops: legume after cereal
• Maintain soil pH between 6.0-7.5

💧 Water Management

• Use drip irrigation - saves 40% water
• Mulch with straw to reduce evaporation
• Water early morning or late evening
• Collect rainwater in farm ponds

🐛 Pest Control (Organic)

• Neem oil spray for aphids & whiteflies
• Marigold border to repel nematodes
• Pheromone traps for fruit borers
• Jeevamrutham for soil health

🌾 Harvest Tips

• Harvest at right moisture (14% for grains)
• Dry crops on tarpaulin, not road
• Store in hermetic bags to prevent pests
• Check mandi prices before selling

💲 Money-Saving Tips

• Buy seeds from govt outlets (cheaper)
• Use Soil Health Card to avoid excess fertilizer
• Form FPO group for better prices
• Apply for KCC loan at 4% interest
